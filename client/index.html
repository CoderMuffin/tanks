How to play

Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move the tank. W and S (or up and down) will move the tank forward and back, and A and D (or left and right) will spin the tank. Use Space to fire

About

This game was made by CoderMuffin :D

Muffin tanks is a 3D, online tanks game where you can shoot each other and objects that are affected by physics, inspired by Wii tanks.
